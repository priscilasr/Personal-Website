Therefore, I have been surrounded by like-minded people for my entire life, and it's only natural that I am following them down the same path.

I am currently in my Junior year at Texas A&M and I am a member of Theta Tau Xi Gamma Fraternity. In the future, I would love to find a job as a Security Software Developer and expand my Father's business from Mexico into the United States.

I'm very excited to see where this path takes me and what lies ahead in the near future. There are tremendous opportunities in the computer science and cybersecurity fields.
